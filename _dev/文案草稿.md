## 01 簡單光板 Simple Panel

**desc**：除了燈光，也可以用一塊「會發光的物體」當主光，除了可以任意制定形狀外，效果也很自然。
**tip**：發光物體面積越大，影子越柔。

*en desc*: Besides lamps, a glowing object can act as the key light. You can shape it however you like, and the result looks natural.
*en tip*: The larger the glowing surface, the softer the shadow.

## 02 一般棚燈 Studio

**desc**：標準的主光、輔光、輪廓光三點打光。主光的目的為照亮、輔光的目的為提亮暗部、輪廓光則能夠把角色從背景裡凸顯出來。
**tip**：打光要維持亮暗的主次感，主光一定要比輔光亮，有亮有暗場景才會有層次與立體感。

*en desc*: The standard three-point setup. The key lights the subject, the fill lifts the shadows, and the rim separates the character from the background.
*en tip*: Keep a clear hierarchy between light and dark. The key must be brighter than the fill — a scene needs both to have depth.

## 03 天使光 Angel Light

**desc**：頭頂一塊很大的發光物體壓下來，下方擺一塊反光板把光補回臉。皮膚乾淨、陰影邊際柔和。
**tip**：透過底部的地板顏色，可以改變頂部光源透過地板反射到角色身上的效果，地板顏色越亮，反光越強，反之越深則越吃光。

*en desc*: A large glowing panel presses down from above, with a bounce board below returning light to the face. Clean skin, soft shadow edges.
*en tip*: The floor colour changes how much of the top light bounces back onto the character. A brighter floor bounces more, a darker one absorbs more.

## 04 網美 Beauty Ring

**desc**：發光物體的形狀可以參考現實世界的光源形狀，如網美燈、泡沫板等，也會得到接近真實世界的效果。
**tip**：透過發光物體的形狀，改變角色的眼睛反射出來的光點形狀，也能呈現不同的角色性格。

*en desc*: Glowing objects can copy the shape of real-world sources such as ring lights or foam boards, which brings you close to a real-world result.
*en tip*: The shape of the glowing object changes the shape of the catchlight in the eyes, which can suggest a different personality.

## 05 網紅 Influencer

**desc**：使用模型替代燈條，並將角色放在環繞的場景內，把溢出的光反射回來。
**tip**：參考既有網紅的影片，去安排燈條的排佈，還原出喜歡的燈光效果。

*en desc*: Model geometry replaces the light strips, with the character placed inside a surrounding set that bounces the spill back.
*en tip*: Look at real streamer footage and copy how the strips are arranged to rebuild a look you like.

## 06 憂鬱 Melancholy

**desc**：改變燈光的顏色來體現出角色的氛圍。
**tip**：燈光強度與對比越低，整體的感覺越抑鬱；越亮，對比越強，則越鮮明活潑。

*en desc*: Change the colour of the light to express the character's mood.
*en tip*: Lower intensity and contrast feel more subdued; brighter with stronger contrast feels livelier.

## 07 靈異 Eerie

**desc**：把發光物體放到角色下方往上打，光的方向一反轉，陰影就會落在平常不會出現的位置。
**tip**：人眼習慣光來自上方，由下往上的光會本能地讓人覺得不安，這是恐怖片常用的手法。

*en desc*: Place the glowing object below the character, firing upward. Reverse the direction of the light and shadows land where they normally never do.
*en tip*: Our eyes expect light from above, so light from below instinctively feels unsettling. It is a staple of horror films.

## 08 百葉窗 Venetian Blinds

**desc**：用日光搭配遮擋物，光穿過葉片後在角色身上留下條紋。遮擋物本身不發光，只負責擋掉部分光線。
**tip**：遮擋物離角色越近，條紋邊緣越銳利，越遠則越模糊；透過調整遮擋物的透明度，可以適當改變光的穿透性，讓陰影不會死黑。

*en desc*: Sun light paired with a blocker. The light passes through the slats and leaves stripes on the character. The blocker does not emit, it only removes part of the light.
*en tip*: The closer the blocker sits to the character, the sharper the stripe edges; further away is softer. Adjusting the blocker's transparency changes how much light passes through, so the shadows never go pure black.

## 09 夕陽氛圍 Sunset Mood

**desc**：用一顆巨大的漸層發光球罩住場景，並設為攝影機不可見。畫面上看不到光源，但它的顏色會落在角色的高光與反射上。
**tip**：發光物體設為攝影機不可見後，就能自由調整大小與亮度而不影響構圖，是控制環境色最方便的做法。

*en desc*: A huge gradient sphere wraps the scene and is set to be invisible to the camera. You never see the source, but its colour lands in the highlights and reflections.
*en tip*: Once a glowing object is invisible to camera you can change its size and brightness freely without affecting the composition. It is the easiest way to control ambient colour.

## 10 未來感 Futuristic

**desc**：用兩個對比色從左右對打，中間留下一條暗部。角色的立體感來自兩色的交界，而不是亮度差。
**tip**：冷暖對比最強烈；兩側強度刻意做得不對稱，畫面會比左右平均更耐看。

*en desc*: Two contrasting colours from opposite sides, leaving a dark seam down the middle. The sense of form comes from where the colours meet, not from brightness.
*en tip*: Warm against cool gives the strongest contrast. Making the two sides deliberately uneven holds the eye better than an even split.

## 11 漸層 Gradient

**desc**：整組沒有使用任何燈光，光線全部來自一片巨大的漸層發光板，是「物體也能當光源」最極端的例子。
**tip**：漸層兩端的顏色決定整體色調，改材質裡的顏色就能換掉一整套氛圍，不需要重新佈光。

*en desc*: No lamps at all. Every bit of light comes from one huge gradient panel, the most extreme case of an object acting as a light source.
*en tip*: The two ends of the gradient set the overall colour. Editing them in the material swaps the whole mood without relighting.

## 12 復古 Retro

**desc**：三盞暖色聚光燈打進一團薄霧，光線經過霧氣散射之後，光束本身就變得可見。
**tip**：霧的濃度越高，光束越明顯，但算圖時間也會拉長，濃度是氛圍與效率之間的取捨。

*en desc*: Three warm spot lights fire into thin fog. Once the light scatters through it, the beams themselves become visible.
*en tip*: Denser fog makes the beams more visible but lengthens render times. Density is a trade-off between mood and speed.
